Research

Situation: Doing research (discovering something that is beyond what we already know) is expensive.

Merit System (of faculty):  
Given the amount of scarce resources allotted to you, produce as many substantial research findings and publish the results, e.g in reputable journals, as contribution to humanity's total knowledge base.

By-product: Boosts the reputation of the school/company/university

Reference:
1) Lewis Thomas' "Medicine: The Youngest Science"
