# Deutsch: German Language
## Notfall: Emergency
<b>POLIZEI! (pollitsigh):</b> Police!<br/>
<b>HALTET DEN DIEB! (hahltert dayn deep):</b> Halt Thief!<br/>

## Notrufnummern (Emergency telephone numbers)
### In Germany: 
<b>Police:</b> 110<br/>
<b>Fire:</b> 112<br/>
<b>Ambulance:</b> 112<br/>
