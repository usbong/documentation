## Additional Notes:
<b>können</b> : "infinitive", i.e. dictionary form of the verb<br/>

<b>können</b> is also used with imperative, i.e. COMMANDs;<br/>
however, we put the personal pronoun after it, instead of before.<br/>
<br/>
## Example
<b>Können wir!</b><br/>
We can!<br/>
